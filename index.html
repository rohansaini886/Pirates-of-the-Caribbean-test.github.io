Jack Sparrow

Captain Jack Sparrow is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the Pirates of the Caribbean film series. The character was created by screenwriters Ted Elliott and Terry Rossio and is portrayed by Johnny Depp.

Hector Barbossa

Captain Hector Barbossa is the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise, appearing in all films of the series. Starting out as a villainous undead pirate in The Curse of the Black Pearl, the character dies at the end of the film.

Elizabeth Swann

Elizabeth Swann is a fictional character in the Pirates of the Caribbean film series. She appears in The Curse of the Black Pearl and three of its sequels, Dead Man's Chest, At World's End and Dead Men Tell No Tales.

Joshamee Gibbs

Joshamee Gibbs is a fictional character in the Pirates of the Caribbean film series. Alongside Jack Sparrow and Hector Barbossa, he is one of the few characters to appear in every film. Gibbs is portrayed by Kevin McNally.
